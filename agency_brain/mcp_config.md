# MCP: Reality Execution Layer

To bridge the agency's digital brain with the real world, the following MCP connections are established/recommended:

## 1. Research & Data (External)
- **Google Search MCP**: For the Trend Analyst to find latest market movements.
- **Context7 / Documentation MCP**: To retrieve technical specs for new product benchmarks.

## 2. Design & Creation (Tools)
- **Image Gen MCP**: Lead Designer uses this for visual asset creation.
- **Filesystem MCP**: For all agents to read/write their thoughts and artifacts to the workspace.

## 3. Communication & Sales (Market)
- **Email/Slack MCP**: For CD to communicate with potential clients or send product pitches.
- **E-commerce/Store MCP**: To automate the listing of new products (Conceptual).

## 4. Automation & Workflows
- **Terminal/Command MCP**: For the Product Producer to run build scripts, verify code, or package design files.
- **Sequential Thinking MCP**: For complex problem-solving during the design phase.
