# Agency Ontology: The Design Identity

## 1. Core Essence
- **Sophistication (세련됨)**: High aesthetic standards, refined details, and a premium feel in every output.
- **Minimalism (미니멀리즘)**: Clarity over clutter. We believe that simplicity is the ultimate sophistication.
- **Innovation (혁신)**: Leading the market by thinking ahead, not following. Using tech and design to solve problems differently.

## 2. Business Logic
- **Build Portfolio**: Constantly generating high-end design assets to maintain a "World-Class" image.
- **Audience Appeal**: Targeting high-intent clients who value brand identity and premium aesthetics.
- **Productization**: Designing original products (digital/physical) based on agency's design philosophy and selling them to a global market.

## 3. Values
- **Integrity**: Honesty in design and delivery.
- **Efficiency**: Lean processes for maximum creative output.
- **Visionary**: Focusing on the long-term impact of design.
